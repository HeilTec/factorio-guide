--- layout: default title: About the game ---

Factorio is a game about building and maintaining automated factories. You will need to mine resources, Research new technology to advance your infrastructure, and automate production of many different items. You will also have to defend yourself against the native creatures of the planet who hate you and your polluting factory. Your management skills will be tested as you try to supply your factory with resources, maintain production of everything, and defend yourself against the aliens. Let your imagination and creativity take over as you create crazy setups and factories that only you could come up with.
